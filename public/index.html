INTRODUCTION

About VinBrain

Up to 2023, about two-thirds of the world lacks access to essential medical diagnostics. VinBrain is here to change that. Our mission is clear: to infuse AI and IoT into healthcare to improve people’s lives and productivity. At the core of our efforts is DrAid™ AI.

Enhance Teleradiology

DrAid™ AI is more than tech; it is our commitment to healthcare equity. We know that cost and access to advanced medical tools hinder care. The integration of AI into DrAid™ AI PACS enables healthcare facilities to perform effective teleradiology consultations regardless of distance and time.

Comprehensive Healthcare

With Comprehensive Screening, Oncology Diagnosis and Treatment, and Social Impact Diseases Screening, all powered by AI, we redefine healthcare solutions. Our belief is simple: AI can solve global healthcare challenges.

Proven Results

We have achieved significant milestones. Our CXR Screening has US FDA Clearance for adult Chest X-Ray cases suggestive of pneumothorax, a distinction for Vietnam. We are also seeking US FDA Clearance for Liver Cancer Diagnosis and Treatment. Our AI products meet ISO 13485:2016 and ISO 9001:2015 standards. DrAid™ AI has been deployed in over 182 hospitals in Vietnam and globally.

A Global Collaboration

DrAid™ AI is the creature of partnerships with top hospitals and academic institutions worldwide. We have partnered with leading organizations like Stanford Center for Artificial Intelligence in Medicine & Imaging, Harvard Medical University, and the University of San Diego, California, the University of Toronto. We have also collaborated with tech giants like Microsoft and NVIDIA. Our extensive dataset comprises nearly 5.4 million data points from a diverse range of countries around the world, including Vietnam, the U.S., and India, among others.

A Vision for Tomorrow

In August 2023, we introduced DrAid™ Enterprise Data Solution, driving healthcare transformation through AI and Big Data. It streamlines data management, empowering healthcare organizations to enhance efficiency and make informed decisions. At VinBrain, we are not just changing healthcare; we are redefining possibilities. Our commitment to healthcare equity, innovation, and collaboration makes us a game-changer. Join us on this journey to push the boundaries of what AI can achieve in healthcare and make a difference, one patient at a time.
